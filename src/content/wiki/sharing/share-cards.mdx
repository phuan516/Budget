## What share cards are

Share cards are exportable image snapshots of your spending data for a given month. Each card is designed to be shared — to a group chat, a social post, or just saved for your own records. All spending figures on share cards **exclude carry-over transactions** so the numbers reflect only what you actually spent that month.

## The five card formats

### Monthly
A summary of the month: income, total spending, the biggest category, and how much you had left over (or overspent). Good for a quick "how was my month?" check-in.

### Wrapped
An annual summary — best used in December or at year-end. Shows total income and spending for the year, top categories, and the month with the most spending.

### Year to Date
Like Year Review but calculated from January 1 through the current month. Shows cumulative totals so you can see how the year is tracking.

### Breakdown
A visual category breakdown for the month — each category as a bar or slice showing its share of total variable spending. Sized for social media stories (9:16).

### Streak
Your current no-spend streak — the longest run of consecutive days with zero spending — alongside your biggest and cheapest spending day stats for the month.

## How to export a card

1. On the **Transactions** tab (or the Overview), open the share menu.
2. Select the card format you want.
3. A preview renders. Tap **Download** to save it as a PNG, or tap **Share** to send it directly via the system share sheet on mobile.

## Privacy

Share cards include spending totals and category names but do not include individual transaction notes or merchant names. Review the preview before sharing to make sure you are comfortable with what is visible.

> **Tip:** The Streak card is most satisfying at the end of a good month. Pair it with the Monthly Recap for a complete picture before you share.
